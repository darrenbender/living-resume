Product Counsel — concept demo

An interview prototype for a Product Counsel role — an internal legal-ops issue-spotting workspace covering current locations (US & Canada) and anticipated expansion (UK, EU) with sample jurisdiction briefs; product workstreams for Caper Carts, Storefront Pro, and a Claude-powered assistant; a Law Library study map of the e-commerce legal stack; and a résumé/fit section. Human-in-the-loop by design; it drafts and routes issues but never gives final legal advice. All legal content is illustrative sample material, not legal advice.
